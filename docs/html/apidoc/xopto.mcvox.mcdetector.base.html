cl_options(mc: xopto.mcbase.mcobject.McObject) → str[source]¶

Returns the OpenCL options of the detectors.

If the detectors for the top or bottom sample surface or the detector of specular reflection are specified, this function activates (not the dummy default layout:py:class:SurfaceLayoutDefault), the corresponding OpenCL options that enable the corresponding surface detector, i.e. MC_USE_TOP_DETECTOR for the detector at the top sample surface, MC_USE_BOTTOM_DETECTOR for the detector at the bottom sample surface and MC_USE_SPECULAR_DETECTOR for detector of specular reflections.
